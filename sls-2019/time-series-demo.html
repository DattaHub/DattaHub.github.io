Simulate AR(1) process

\[ x_{t} = \phi x_{t-1} + \epsilon_t, \; \epsilon_t \sim N(0, \nu) \]

phi = 0.9; nu = 1; T = 500
x = numeric(T)
for(t in 1:(T-1)){
  x[t+1] = phi*x[t]+rnorm(1,0,nu)
}

Different \(\phi\)

Simple HMM

\[ \begin{aligned} y_{t} &=x_{t}+\nu_{t} \\ x_{t} & \leftarrow AR(1 | \theta) \end{aligned} \]

Simple Stochastic Volatility

\[ \begin{align} y_t & \sim N(0, \sigma_t^2) \\ \sigma_t & \sim \exp(\mu + x_t) \\ x_t & \sim AR(1 \mid \theta) \; \theta = (\phi, \nu). \end{align} \] Equivalently

  • \(y_t^2 = \sigma_t^2 \kappa_t\), where \(\kappa_t \sim \chi^2_1\).
  • Take \(z_t = \log(y_t^2)/2 = \log(|y_t|)\).
  • The \(z_t\) are now the new observed quantities.

\[ \begin{align} z_t & = \mu + x_t + \nu_t, \quad \text{where} \; \nu_t = \log(\kappa_t)/2\\ x_t & \sim AR(1 \mid \theta); \; \theta = (\phi, \nu) \end{align} \]
